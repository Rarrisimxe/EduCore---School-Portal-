# EduCore-School-Portal-
EduCore is a comprehensive school management system designed to streamline academic and administrative operations in one centralized platform.

It includes the following functions:

User Management: Secure login system with role-based access for students, staff, and administrators.
Student Management: Add, update, and manage student records such as name, ID, course, email, and roll number.
Staff Management: Handles teacher/staff profiles, roles, and assigned responsibilities.
Attendance Management: Records daily attendance and tracks student presence/absence.
Results Management: Stores exam results and academic performance data for each student.
Course Management: Organizes subjects, classes, and academic programs.
Timetable Management: Creates and manages class schedules and subject timings.
Email/Notification System: Sends important updates, announcements, and alerts to students and staff.
Virtual Classroom Integration: Supports online classes, allowing teachers to conduct live sessions and share materials remotely.
Reports & Analytics: Generates performance reports for students and staff.
Charts & Visualization: Displays attendance and result performance using graphs and charts for better analysis.
Dashboard System: Separate dashboards for admin, staff, and students for easy navigation and access to relevant features.   

EduCore is a school management system that brings all academic and administrative tasks into one platform.  It handles user access, student and staff records, attendance, results, courses, timetables, and communication. It also supports online classes and provides dashboards, reports, and simple analytics for better tracking and management.
